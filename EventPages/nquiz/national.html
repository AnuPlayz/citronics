Rules

1. It will be based on the pattern of popular game show Kaun Banega Crorepati (KBC) and played by software.
2. Candidate on HOT SEAT would be decided by Fastest Finger First Round, played on player's mobile phone.
3. In case of team play, only one of the player is allowed to play the game
4. Total number of Questions in Game Show will be 15
5. Each question consists of 4 options, in which one is correct.
6. Timer is associated with every question to answer.
7. There will be four life lines as follows:
(a) Audience Poll
(b) Expert Advice
(c) Flip Question
(d) 50-50
8. In case of any dispute, the organizer decision would be considered as final.


Eligiblity Criteria

  1. Participant must be 18+.
  2. There is no refunding policy.
