Description

Carrom is a tabletop game of Indian origin in which players use striker in order to knock the carrom men to the corners of the board. In carrom two, three or four people can play simultaneously. "Don't bother, Just play"

Rules

1. This is a four player games
2. Carrom men of either colour
a. The queen gives 5 points.
b. The black gives 1 points.
c. The white gives 2 points.
3. To get queen points, one needs to put a carrom man of any colour in the same pocket after the queen on player fails to "cover" the queen center of the board.
4. The first player to reach 25
5. If no player reaches 25 points, the player with the highest points is declared the winner. If the scores are tied, a tie select a color and are only allowed to pocket carrom men of the other color on the rebound.
6. When placing the striker on the board to shoot, the striker must touch both 'base lines' (see left diagram), either covering the touching it at all. The striker may not touch the diagonal arrow line.
7. Shooting styles are very personal fine as long as you 'flick' the striker and don't push it.
8. The Amount paid is non-refundable.
