Rules

1. This is a team game. All teams must have at least 5 members.
2. Start with a clue.
3. Teams must stay together during the Treasure Hunt and are encouraged to work together to solve the clues.
4. Team completing in least timing will be awarded.
5. Disqualification will result from any of the following.
a. Damaging any property.
b. Interfering with other teams or their members in any way.
c. Copying from other teams.
d. Stealing from other teams.
6. No dividing up to look for clues.
7. If you are stuck, only team captain may ask for a clue from volunteer.


Eligiblity Criteria

  1. Participant must be 18+.
  2. There is no refunding policy.
