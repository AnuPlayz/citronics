Rules

1. Each performer will get 3 to 5 minutes. So, prepare accordingly.
2. All participants of the entries should be atleast 13 years old and above.
3. All the participants have to Register on website with registration fees.
4. We will provide you with - Sound board, Speakers, Mics, and Cable.
5. When you are next, be ready.
6. Please be quiet and show respect for all other performers. Applaud for everyone.
7. Be respectful to the venue and equipment.
8. Please stay after your performance till the results get declared.
9. Your content should be free from any kind of sexual and religious vulgarity.
10. The official entries shall be provided by the organizers with a schedule of activities.
11. Those who are absent/late for their designated blocking time will not be allowed to block on stage at a later time.
12. A panel of judges shall determine the winners based on the foregoing criteria.
13. Any violation of the foregoing rules may cause 3 points deduction per violation on the final score. The decision of the panel shall be final and irrevocable.


Eligiblity Criteria

  1. Participant must be 18+.
  2. There is no refunding policy.
