Rules

1. The event will comprise of three rounds. The Minimum Team members should be 2 and Maximum Team members will be 4. Participants have to sell the given item to judges as well as the audience. First round will be a power snatcher round where the team has to collect powers (resources). Each team will be given 500 points. Resources window will open after the power snatcher round in which the team has to buy resources for establishing their startup. Team have to build their stall for selling the product with the help of allotted points and resources brought. Each item will have different points. Team will get maximum 2 chances to convince the audience to sell their product. Audience can also engage in between the event. If any participant found misbehaving or in any act of cheating/violating rules, he/she will immediately be disqualified.


Eligiblity Criteria

  1. Participant must be 18+.
  2. There is no refunding policy.
